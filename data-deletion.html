Account & Data Deletion Policy

Price of Trust by Mind Arena

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Price of Trust account and all associated data, send an email to:

Email: infomindarena@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - Price of Trust

Include in your email:

  • Your username or user ID (if known)
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

What Data Will Be Deleted

Immediately Deleted:

  • Account Information: Your Firebase authentication account and email
  • Player Profile: Username, thought power, games played, and games won statistics
  • Leaderboard Entries: Your position and scores on the leaderboard
  • Device Tokens: FCM tokens used for push notifications

Data Retention:

  • Game Messages: Messages you sent in completed games will remain visible to other players as part of their game history. Your username will be removed from these messages.
  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement. This data cannot be linked back to you.
  • Legal Compliance: In rare cases, certain data may be retained if required by law or for fraud prevention.

Timeline

  • Processing Time: Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days
  • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your game progress, statistics, and data will be lost forever.
