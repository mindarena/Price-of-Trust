Account & Data Deletion Policy

Price of Trust by Mind Arena

How to Request Account Deletion

You have two options to delete your Price of Trust account and all associated data:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the Price of Trust app
  2. Go to the Home screen
  3. Long-press your username at the top of the screen
  4. Select "Delete Account" from the menu
  5. Confirm your decision

This is the fastest method and processes your deletion immediately.

Option 2: Email Request

Email: infomindarena@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - Price of Trust

Include in your email:

  • Your username or user ID (if known)
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

Email requests are processed within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Permanently Deleted:

  • Account Information: Your Firebase authentication account and email address
  • Player Profile: Username, Thought Power (TP), games played, and games won statistics
  • Leaderboard Entries: Your position and scores on the global leaderboard
  • Device Tokens: FCM tokens used for push notifications
  • Active Game Data: You will be removed from any ongoing games
  • Game State: Your balance, loans, transactions, and votes in active games
  • Rate Limits: Any rate limiting data associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained:

  • Completed Game Messages: Messages you sent in completed games may remain visible to other players as part of their game history (your username will be anonymized)
  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Legal Compliance: Certain data may be retained if required by law, fraud prevention, or security purposes

Timeline

  • In-App Deletion: Processed immediately when you confirm deletion
  • Email Requests: Processed within 30 days of receiving your request
  • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted (for email requests)
  • Immediate Effect: Once deleted, you will be logged out and cannot access your account
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted:
  • All your Thought Power (TP) will be lost
  • Your leaderboard ranking will be removed
  • All game statistics (games played, games won) will be deleted
  • You will be removed from any active games
  • You cannot recover your account or data
  • You will need to create a new account to play again

Alternative: Disable Notifications Only

If you only want to stop receiving notifications without deleting your account:

  1. Open the app and go to the Home screen
  2. Toggle off the "Notifications" switch
  3. Or disable notifications in your device settings

This keeps your account and progress intact while stopping all notifications.
